## THE SESSION SCRIPT

Begin writing the complete session now, following this exact therapeutic arc. Adjust the depth and length of each phase to fill precisely {{sessionDuration}} minutes of spoken audio.

---

**PHASE 1 — ORIENTING INTRODUCTION (2-3 minutes)**

Open with warmth, safety, and curiosity. Welcome the listener by first name if known. Set a tone of permission and possibility — nothing is forced, everything unfolds naturally. Create positive expectancy. Use Ericksonian permissive language throughout: "you may find...", "you might notice...", "and as you begin to..."

Let them know this is their time. No obligations, no performance. Simply an opportunity to receive something deeply nourishing.

---

**PHASE 2 — INDUCTION (3-5 minutes)**

Guide the listener into a deep, comfortable trance state using:
- Breath awareness and pacing
- Progressive physical relaxation (feet → legs → torso → shoulders → face)
- Ericksonian language patterns (double binds, embedded commands, truisms)
- Gentle counting deepeners

{{#if representationalSystem}}
Calibrate all induction language to the {{representationalSystem}} system:
- If VISUAL: "notice the soft darkness behind your eyes", "see yourself sinking deeper"
- If AUDITORY: "listen to the silence between sounds", "hear your inner voice growing quieter"
- If KINESTHETIC: "feel the warm heaviness spreading through your limbs", "notice the weight releasing"
{{/if}}

Example Ericksonian patterns to weave in naturally:
- "And you don't need to try to relax — in fact, the very act of trying to relax makes it harder, and so you can simply... let go."
- "I wonder if you'll notice which part of your body relaxes first."
- "Whether your eyes remain open or closed matters less than the fact that your mind can go exactly where it needs to go."

---

**PHASE 3 — DEEPENING (2-3 minutes)**

Deepen the trance state significantly using:
- Staircase or elevator metaphor (descending deeper with each step/floor)
- Nested suggestions within imagery
- Time distortion: "a minute can feel like an hour of deep rest"
- Direct suggestion: "ten times deeper... twenty times more relaxed..."

Confirm they are safely, beautifully in trance. Establish unconscious signal (e.g., "and your unconscious mind will let you know it's ready by allowing your body to feel completely, perfectly still").

---

**PHASE 4 — RESOURCE ACTIVATION (3-4 minutes)**

Guide the listener back to their positive anchoring memory: "{{positiveAnchoringMemory}}"

Make it fully vivid and multi-sensory. What do they see around them? What sounds? What does the air feel like? What is the quality of that positive feeling in their body?

As the feeling reaches its peak intensity, install an anchor: "Let this feeling fill every cell of your body... and as it reaches its peak, notice that you can carry this state with you always... it is yours, available in an instant."

Layer this anchored state into the work that follows — return to it whenever needed to power the change work.

---

**PHASE 5 — NESTED LOOP METAPHORS (4-6 minutes)**

Open 2-3 metaphorical stories that mirror the user's transformation, using their interests ({{interests}}) and life context ({{workLifeEnvironment}}) as the story setting. Do NOT close these stories yet — let them remain open, suspended in the listener's unconscious.

The metaphors should operate at multiple levels simultaneously:
- Surface: a story about their interest (hiking, cooking, music, coding, etc.)
- Deeper: a metaphor for their specific challenge ({{mainIssue}})
- Deepest: a story about the unconscious discovering a new way

Example structure if interests include nature or hiking:
"There is a traveller who has walked the same path for years... and one day, they notice a fork they had never seen before... and as they pause at that fork, something shifts... but I'll come back to what happened next in a moment..."

IMPORTANT: Leave the stories open. You will close them after the core change work.

---

**PHASE 6 — CORE CHANGE WORK (8-15 minutes)**

This is the heart of the session. Based on the diagnostic and intake data, layer the selected therapeutic techniques naturally and seamlessly.

Always begin with:
**Softening the Pattern:** "That part of you that has been carrying {{mainIssue}} all this time... it has been working so hard... doing what it believed was protecting you... and today, we simply invite it to rest."

Then execute the primary technique sequence:

For the core issue ({{mainIssue}}) present for {{issueDuration}}:
Acknowledge the duration. Acknowledge how much effort the person has invested. Create compassion for the part that maintained the pattern. Then begin the transformation.

{{#if repeatingThoughts}}
**Thought Pattern Work:** The repeating thought "{{repeatingThoughts}}" — shrink it, change its voice, move it far away. Install a counter-thought using submodality shifts. Make the new perspective vivid, close, and real.
{{/if}}

{{#if negativeBeliefs}}
**Belief Reimprinting:** The belief "{{negativeBeliefs}}" — trace it back to when it first formed. Gently revisit that moment. The younger self needed protection, but they deserved understanding. Offer the understanding they needed. Watch the belief dissolve and reconstitute as something true and empowering.
{{/if}}

{{#if hasInnerConflict}}
**Parts Integration:**
"There is a part of you that wants {{desiredEmotionalShift}}... and there is another part that has reasons to stay where you are... and both parts have been trying to help you... I'd like to invite both of these parts to step forward..."

Guide a full parts dialogue — each part speaks, expresses its positive intention, and is met with understanding. Then facilitate a merger: "And now these two parts, both wanting what is best for you, slowly move toward each other... and as they meet... something new is born... a unified knowing of what you truly are..."
{{/if}}

**Somatic Release:**
Direct healing attention to the {{bodyLocation}}: "Notice that sensation in your {{bodyLocation}}... give it a colour, a temperature, a texture... now imagine a warm, golden light entering that area from above... dissolving, releasing, transforming that old energy into something lighter... freer..."
{{/if}}

Give it to them now..."
{{/if}}

Close this phase with: "And your unconscious mind has received everything it needs to begin the changes that are already occurring beneath the surface of awareness... changes you may notice immediately... or gradually... or suddenly one day in a situation that used to be difficult, you will simply respond differently... and that will feel exactly right."

**PHASE 7 — IDENTITY INSTALLATION (3-4 minutes)**

Shift the transformation from behavioural to identity level. The change is not just something they DO — it is something they ARE.

"This is not just a new behaviour. This is who you are becoming. Who you already are, beneath the old pattern. The real you — the version of you that was always there, waiting."

{{#if keyAffirmations}}
Weave these affirmations naturally into the identity work, making them feel like they arise from within rather than being imposed:
{{keyAffirmations}}

Do NOT say "repeat after me." Instead, make them feel as if the listener's own deeper self is discovering these truths for the first time.
{{/if}}

"And as this new identity settles into every cell of your body, into the neural pathways of your brain, into the story you tell yourself about who you are... notice how natural it feels... because it IS natural... it is the truth of you."

---

**PHASE 8 — BEHAVIOURAL REHEARSAL (3-4 minutes)**

Create a vivid mental rehearsal of the target behaviour change: "{{behaviorToChange}}"

Guide the listener through a specific, detailed scenario where the old trigger is present ({{triggers}}) but they respond with the new pattern. Make it fully sensory and embodied.

Use the life context ({{workLifeEnvironment}}) to make the scenario immediately relevant. They see it, hear it, feel it. They notice how different it feels to respond this way. They feel the satisfaction, the confidence, the ease.

"Run this experience through your nervous system at least three times... each time it becomes more automatic... more natural... more simply who you are..."

---

**PHASE 9 — FUTURE PACING (3-5 minutes)**

Project the listener 3-6 months into the future where the transformation is complete.

Use their exact vision: "{{successVision}}"

"See yourself in that future... look at yourself through your own eyes in that moment... where are you?... who is with you?... what does your body feel like from the inside?... notice the {{desiredEmotionalShift}} that is simply your natural state now..."

Make it extraordinarily vivid. The smell of the air. The quality of light. The feeling in the chest. The subtle smile that comes naturally now.

Then bring this future self back as a resource: "And this future version of you... wants to give your present self a gift... a message... a feeling... receive it now... let it travel back through time into this moment... into this body..."

---

**PHASE 10 — INTEGRATION (2-3 minutes)**

Close the nested metaphor loops from Phase 5. Each story reaches its resolution — the traveller finds the new path, the cook discovers the perfect combination, the musician finds the note. The resolutions mirror the user's own transformation.

Then offer a post-hypnotic suggestion: "In the days and weeks ahead, you may find moments of unexpected calm... unexpected confidence... unexpected clarity... your unconscious mind continues this work even now, even as we speak, even as you sleep tonight..."

Acknowledge the unconscious: "Everything your unconscious mind needed to receive today, it has received. Everything that needed to shift has already begun to shift. Trust the process. Trust yourself."

## WRITING GUIDELINES

1. **CRITICAL Length & Word-Count Budget:** You MUST generate a complete, full-length, word-for-word script that contains AT LEAST **{{targetWordCount}}** words. This is an absolute hard floor. Do not write a summary, do not use placeholder descriptions (e.g. "[continue counting]"), and do not abbreviate. You must write out every single spoken word in full detail.
To achieve this length, allocate the target word count across the phases as follows:
   - Phase 1 (Orienting Introduction): ~5% (approx. **{{targetWordCount_5}}** words) - Set safe expectations, Ericksonian permissiveness.
   - Phase 2 & 3 (Induction & Deepening): ~20% (approx. **{{targetWordCount_20}}** words) - Extensive breath pacing, progressive relaxation of every body part, staircase/deepener countdowns.
   - Phase 4 & 5 (Resource Activation & Metaphors): ~20% (approx. **{{targetWordCount_20}}** words) - Extremely vivid, multi-sensory resource memories and multi-layered metaphorical stories.
   - Phase 6 (Core Change Work): ~35% (approx. **{{targetWordCount_35}}** words) - Thorough therapeutic/NLP change work, parts integration dialog, somatic release, or timeline healing.
   - Phase 7 & 8 & 9 (Identity, Rehearsal, Future Pacing): ~15% (approx. **{{targetWordCount_15}}** words) - Identity-level integration, detailed situational roleplay, and future projections.
   - Phase 10 & 11 (Integration & Emergence): ~5% (approx. **{{targetWordCount_5}}** words) - Suspending metaphors, final suggestions, and energizing or sleeping emergence.

2. **Programmatic Pauses:** Hypnotherapy requires deep, slow-paced pacing. You MUST weave explicit pause markers in the exact format `[pause: Ns]` where N is the duration in seconds (e.g., `[pause: 2s]`, `[pause: 3s]`, `[pause: 5s]`). Use:
   - `[pause: 1s]` to `[pause: 2s]` between paragraphs or normal breathing cycles.
   - `[pause: 3s]` to `[pause: 4s]` after deep relaxation commands or countdown steps.
   - `[pause: 5s]` to `[pause: 6s]` inside the core change work, somatic release, and future pacing visualization to let the unconscious absorb suggestions.
   Do NOT use any other brackets or stage directions, ONLY the `[pause: Ns]` format.

3. **Voice:** Calm, warm, authoritative but never harsh. Gentle pauses implied by ellipses (...) in addition to the explicit pause markers. The voice blends Erickson's permissive indirectness with Robbins' energising conviction.

4. **Language:** Use "you" and "your" throughout. Second person, present tense. No clinical jargon. No diagnostic labels. Speak TO the subconscious, not ABOUT it.

5. **Repetition:** Hypnotic suggestion works through strategic repetition. Key phrases and suggestions should appear 2-3 times in varied forms.

6. **Embedded Commands:** Weave direct suggestions inside longer sentences. "You might notice how easy it is to RELAX COMPLETELY right now."

8. **Output:** Written script only. No headers, no commentary, no stage directions (except the allowed `[pause: Ns]` markers). Pure spoken text from beginning to end, with programmatic pauses embedded.
